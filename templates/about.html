Who I Am

I’m a Software Engineer and Data Engineer with a strong background in designing and building enterprise software systems, data platforms, and e-commerce solutions. Over the years, I’ve worked across the full lifecycle of systems—from database design and ETL pipelines to application development, cloud infrastructure, and performance optimization. My focus has always been on creating reliable, scalable systems that solve real business problems.

I specialize in SQL-based systems, data engineering workflows, and backend architecture across technologies like Python, JavaScript, PHP, and Perl. I’ve worked extensively with databases such as SQL Server, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and Snowflake, and built data pipelines, reporting systems, and BI dashboards using tools like Power BI and Tableau. I also work with cloud and DevOps tools including AWS, Docker, and Kubernetes to support modern, scalable deployments.

What drives me most is solving complex problems and turning messy, high-volume data into systems that are structured, efficient, and usable. I enjoy improving performance, modernizing legacy systems, and building solutions that help teams make better decisions through data. Recently, I’ve also been exploring Generative AI, RAG systems, and AI-driven automation to extend what data systems can do.

Outside of work, I enjoy learning new technologies, experimenting with system design ideas, and continuously improving my technical craft. I’m always open to collaborating on interesting projects, discussing architecture, or exploring new opportunities in software and data engineering.
